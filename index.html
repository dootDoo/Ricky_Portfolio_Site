Who am I?

Hello there! I'm an aspiring software engineer with a knack for crafting full-stack applications. My adventure started in early 2022 with a simple python script "Hello World". This initial project led me to explore software developent, and an intoduction to full-stack applications. I gained proficiency in javascript and started learning my tech stack.

As a devoted software student, I am currently honing my skills in full-stack development. I have a particular interest in the design aspect of front-end development and the versatility that JavaScript offers. My journey in the tech world has been significantly enriched by my enrollment in the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology (SAIT) for software development. This experience has allowed me to deepen my understanding of various programming languages, software design patterns, and development methodologies.

In the future, I aspire to contribute to open-source projects and use my skills to solve real-world problems. I am excited about the endless possibilities that the field of software development offers and am eager to explore them to the fullest. My ultimate goal is to leave a positive impact on the tech community and beyond, through my work and dedication to continuous learning and sharing.
